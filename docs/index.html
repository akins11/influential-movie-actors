Topping the charts in awards won, popular movie revenue, endorsement deals and movie production, it’s no surprise that Funke Akindele secures the first position. Both have secured some of the highest and most lucrative endorsement deals in the country, and their contributions over the years cannot go unnoticed. In the fourth and fifth positions are popular artists Toyin Abraham and Ramsey Nouah, who have achieved major box-office success with some of their most recent movies. The sixth position is held by the popular Yoruba actor Odunlade Adekola, who has grown immensely popular over the years, attracting lucrative deals for himself.

Other notable actors in the ranking include Jim Iyke, RMD, Pete Edochie, and Sola Sobowale. They have gained recognition for their versatility, iconic performances, commanding presence, mentorship and ability to bring depth to various characters.
